Our goal is to build a complete solution for 3D Printing in Construction. We aim to make the construction process faster, simpler and more versatile by applying the principles of 3D Printing in constructng buildings. We aim to employ this technology in making the construction process affordable for mass construction. The vision is also to build versatility into the construction process whereby complex architectural shapes could be built with ease using 3D Printing.
