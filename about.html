Our Journey

Tvasta is a company founded by four IIT Madras alumni in 2016. Tvasta focuses on Industrial 3D Printing and is a Technology driven company that builds systems and applications for 3D Printing.

The founders of Tvasta see a lot of potential in the high-end Industrial 3D Printing market and also see a lot of potential for pushing the boundaries of the possibilities using 3D Printing technology.
